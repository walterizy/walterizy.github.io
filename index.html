A wonderful trip - once in a lifetime

Our challenge was to climb both Mount Meru and Mount Kilimanjaro our reward was a 3 day safari. Oreta provided its support, expertise, friendship and enthusiasm. Every member of our party of 5 achieved both summits thanks to Oreta's wonderful Guides and Porters. Oreta's tour consultants, Renalda and Lema, supported us throughout our trip planning, in regular contact by email as we developed our itinerary. .

Jon W
Manchester, England, United Kingdom

Safari and Kilimanjaro

A friend and I booked a 4 day safari and a 7 day Lemosho trek up Kilimanjaro. We had a fantastic experience. Goodluck was our safari guide and he was very knowledgeable and was always very organized and prompt. We had a crew of 19 people to take 3 girls up Kili. We all made it to the summit with the help and encouragement of the whole team. The food was also delicious on both trips -- they were even able to cook vegan for us! We aren't expert trekkers, but we felt very safe .

tinytrinh
Austin, Texas

Lovely guides and porters for a safe successful Kili Climb

Oreta were great from the beginning - very responsive on email and very helpful. We were met at the airport (even though we came in on different flights) and straight away given a briefing by our guide about what to expect on the trek. The trek was well organised, very safety conscious and we couldn't have been treated better. We were cooked 3 meals a day, all delicious (and vegan by request) ...

Sarah C

Amazing Kili Climb & Safari!

My parents and I recently climbed Kilimanjaro and did a Safari with Oreta. From the beginning, Renalda and Lema (the owners of the company) were very responsive, answering any questions we had. This really helped to reduce our worries and fears going into the climb. Once we arrived in Kilimanjaro, Renalda was at the airport to greet us and take us back to the hotel. Renalda and Lema arranged for a free coffee tour for us as we had a free day before we began our climb. .
